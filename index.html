Why Yoga?

Yoga, a mind and body practice

According to the Medical News Today, Yoga is a physical, mental and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India over 5.000 years ago. It involves movement, meditation, and breathing techniques to promote mental and physical well-being.

There are several types of Yoga and many disciplines within the practice. The benefits of Yoga include reducing stress, anxiety, depression, and chronic pain, improving sleep, and enhancing flexibility and quality of life, besides many other physical and mental benefits.
