Why Yoga?

Yoga, a mind and body practice

According to the Medical News Today, Yoga is a physical, mental and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India over 5.000 years ago. It involves movement, meditation, and breathing techniques to promote mental and physical well-being.

There are several types of Yoga and many disciplines within the practice. The benefits of Yoga include reducing stress, anxiety, depression, and chronic pain, improving sleep, and enhancing flexibility and quality of life, besides many other physical and mental benefits.

ASANAS

My favorite Yoga poses

Dhanurasana

Bending back into the shape of a bow to feel energetically locked, loaded, and ready to take aim.

Pose benefits

Increases blood flow to your digestive system, improves posture, relieves mild backaches, fatigue, anxiety, and menstrual discomfort, besides many other perks.

What I like about it

This pose helps us to fight fatigue by giving us an extra boost while stimulating abdominal organs and improving our posture.

Learn more

Parivrtta Janu Sirsasana

Revolved Head-to-Knee Pose, or Parivrtta Janu Sirsasana, offers a deep, vivid stretch to the side body paired with loving and heart-opening movement.

Pose benefits

stretches the spine, shoulders, and hamstrings, stimulates your abdominal organs, such as the liver and kidneys, and improves overall digestion.

What I like about it

This pose is relaxing and energizing at the same time, allowing us to breathe more deeply and stretch more intensely.

Learn more

Salamba Sirsasana

Standing on your head in Salamba Sirsasana (Supported Headstand) strengthens the whole body and calms the brain.

Pose benefits

Improves body awareness, circulation, and posture. It can help reduce swelling in your ankles and feet, boost energy, fight fatigue, and build confidence.

What I like about it

This pose challenges us to approach it mentally first, giving us a new and fresh perspective while being upside down.
